Privacy Policy

PARCHI - Grocery List App · Last updated: July 2026

In short: PARCHI stores your grocery list, history, and preferences only on your own phone. We don't run a server that stores your personal data, and we don't sell your information to anyone.

1. Who we are

PARCHI is a grocery list app designed to help you create shopping lists, share them with shopkeepers or family members, and track your household spending. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

2. Information stored on your device

The following information is saved directly on your phone's browser storage, and is not transmitted to or stored on any server we control:

  • Your grocery list items, categories, and purchase history
  • Calendar and expense tracking records you add
  • Photos you attach to items (compressed and stored on-device only)
  • Your name, mobile number, and address, if you choose to save them for sharing lists

This data stays on your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser's site data will permanently delete it. We have no way to recover it, because we never had a copy.

3. Microphone and voice input

When you use the voice input feature, your device's browser (Google Chrome) sends the audio to Google's speech recognition service to convert it to text. This is handled entirely by your phone's browser, not by PARCHI directly. We do not receive, store, or have access to your voice recordings.

4. Camera and photos

If you choose to attach a photo to a grocery item, PARCHI accesses your camera or photo gallery only when you tap that option. Photos are resized and stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded anywhere by us.

5. Sharing your list

When you share a list with a shopkeeper or another person, PARCHI creates a message (and, for the shopkeeper flow, a link containing your list details) that is sent through your own WhatsApp or messaging app. This happens through your device's normal sharing function - we do not have access to who you send it to or what happens after it's sent.

For the shopkeeper price-confirmation feature, the item list and pricing details are encoded directly into the link itself, rather than stored on a server. To keep this link shorter and easier to share, it may be passed through a third-party link-shortening service (CleanURI). That service can see the encoded link content while generating the short link, but PARCHI does not separately send them any of your personal profile information beyond what's already in that link.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not run advertising or ad tracking in this app
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not require you to create an account or log in
  • We do not track your location

7. Third-party services used

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts and, for voice input, uses Google's speech recognition service through your browser. These services may receive standard technical information (like your IP address) as part of normal web requests, governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Children's privacy

PARCHI is a general household utility app and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this same page with a new "last updated" date.
